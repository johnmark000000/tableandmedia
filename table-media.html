Grand Chase Classic Wiki

Background

The Grand Chase was formally established in the year 1459 after the Kanavan Trackers, who had been dispatched by Queen Anyumena to pursue Kaze'aze, failed to return from their mission. In her final act of desperation, Queen Enaruv would form the Grand Chase, her one last hope, with Elesis, Lire, and Arme handpicked as its founding members by the Queen herself, recruiting them through a secret letter.

Under the guidance of Knight Master Isolet, the three young heroes underwent a series of rigorous trials and quests before they were officially recognized as the Grand Chase, and they have grown in numbers since as warriors with newfound purposes aspired to join the organization.

Characters

The twenty members of the Grand Chase are playable characters in the game. Each has a unique way of fighting and each possesses unique skills in combat.

Originally the game only contained 3 characters: the Knight Elesis, the Archer Lire, and the Magician Arme. As the game expands, more characters have been introduced and each of them has been given extra unlockable Job Change classes to improve their fighting abilities.

Other characters that have been introduced since include the Thief Lass, the Druid Ryan, the Spell Knight Ronan, the Dancer Amy, the Fighter Jin, the Gladiator Sieghart, the Rune Caster Mari, the Stygian Dio, the Wanderer Zero, the Summoner Ley, the Bounty Hunter Rufus, the Phoenix Rin, the Disciple Asin, the Holy Knight Lime, the Captain Edel, the Magi Veigas, the Bloodless Uno, the Chaos Witch Decanee, and most recently, the Operator Ai.
